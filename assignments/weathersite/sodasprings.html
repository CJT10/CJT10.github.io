The Perpetual Tree Fund

by Soda Springs City Council

The Perpetual Tree fund was started in 2007 and has provided our community with a unique way of honoring or remembering a special person or organization. The Perpetual Tree Fund allows individuals, groups, or businesses to purchase a tree or bench to be placed in one of the City Parks in memory of a loved one or to honor a special person or organization. Each tree or bench will be marked with a personalized brass plaque. Tree donations are $200 and a beautiful bench is $1000. A map and the types of trees available will be coming soon. Any questions about the Perpetual Tree Fund, please call City Hall at 547-2600 or visit us at sodaspringsid.com.
