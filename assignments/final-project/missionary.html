Long-term stay suites available!

All suites offer spacious and comfortable lodging with a fully-equipped kitchen, TV, sofa, and eating area.

Temple Inn & Suites offers full-time missionaries the opportunity to have the comforts of home plus the benefits of a hotel.

Our spacious suites feature in-room kitchens where guests can store food, prepare meals, enjoy snacks, and maintain their normal eating routines while away from home. The kitchens are fully-equipped featuring a stovetop, microwave and full-size refrigerator. A toaster and additional dishes, pots, pans, and silverware are available upon request.
