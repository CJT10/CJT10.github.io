Ready-to-use fishing license available on smartphones

by The Preston Citizen

Forgetful anglers can avoid their panic if they realize they have forgotten their fishing licences, as long as they have a smart phone. Fishing licenses can now be purchased and downloaded online. Anglers who already have an account on Fish and Game’s licensing system, can visit the Idaho Department of Fish & Game using a smartphone, and login using a user name and password associated with their account, states the Fish and Game. If they do not have an online account with Idaho Fish and Game, they should tap the “Enroll Now” button and enter the required information to set up an account.
